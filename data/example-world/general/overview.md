# The Forgotten Realm

## Geography
- **Thornhaven Forest**: An ancient woodland shrouded in mist, home to fey creatures and lost ruins
- **Ironforge Peak**: A volcanic mountain where dwarves mine rare metals
- **The Obsidian Tower**: A mysterious structure that appears on no maps, said to contain forbidden knowledge

## Factions
- **The Silver Circle**: An order of mages dedicated to preserving balance
- **Blackthorn Company**: Mercenaries with connections to dark dealings
- **The Council of Elders**: Wise beings who protect ancient knowledge

## Themes
- Redemption through courage
- Balance between progress and tradition
- Hidden truths that change everything
